Art and Soul - Art Therapy

"Art and Soul helped me to meet others that were going through the same things, to hear their story and be encouraged." - Don

What...

Come and experience creating with painting, drawing and mixed media. Creative expression will relax, inspire, and strengthen your artistic spirit. If you are affected by cancer, art can give you a way to talk about your feelings by allowing you to focus on a project and think about something other than the diagnosis. Doing art itself is therapeutic and it is the process, not the product, that is the primary focus. We encourage you to come with a sense of curiosity, adventure, and a willingness to jump in and try something new.
