Biographies:


Kimberly Bailey received her Bachelors of Arts and Science in Art Therapy from The Ohio State University. She is a certified facilitator in Intuitive Painting and the Expressive Arts. Kim is in private practice as a spiritual counselor, Reiki Master and shamanic practitioner. She is currently teaching Healing Art classes at Cancer CAREpoint, Self Appreciation classes for ECH Cancer Center Staff and Intuitive Painting workshops in the community. She has been volunteering at El Camino Hospitalâ€™s Healing Arts program for 18 months and has been co-teaching the 'Art and Soul' program at ECH Cancer Center this past year. Kimberly worked as a registered social worker for 15 years with the elderly, adults, developmentally disabled adults and children. She has lead various women, cancer and children support groups over the past 20 years. Kimberly has been providing therapeutic art to various populations in Michigan and California for 30 years. She has used art to heal herself through chronic pain and breast cancer and has inspired other to use art as a tool for healing themselves.


Meredith Smith holds a Liberal Studies degree with an emphasis in Creative Arts from San Francisco State University. She is a formally trained artist in several mediums. After leaving the restaurant business in 2011, Meredith was a part time student at Pacific Art League in Palo Alto. Studying under local bay area artist, Ray Mendietta, she expanded her expertise in drawing, plein air painting, and portraiture. She has also studied sculpture, abstract art, watercolor and art history. Meredith has been an El Camino Hospital Spiritual Care volunteer since March 2015 and is the ECH Healing Arts Volunteer Coordinator. She has been co-teaching the Art & Soul program in the Cancer Center at El Camino Hospital for the past year. Meredith is a cancer survivor and uses art as a common language to connect with people. She encourages patients and care givers to find their own voice through creativity to help with the healing process of cancer.
